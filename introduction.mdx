## Overview

Tables Guru provides a powerful platform where you organize, manage, and collaborate on all your project documentation. You create structured spaces for docs, tables, and knowledge bases that scale with your team. Access everything from a single dashboard at `https://dashboard.tablesguru.com`.

This centralized approach eliminates scattered files and version conflicts. You build living documentation that updates automatically as your projects evolve.

</Callout>

## Quick Start

Get up and running with Tables Guru in three steps:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create Account" icon="user-plus">
    Visit `https://tablesguru.com` and sign up with your email. Verify instantlyâ€”no credit card required.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Build First Space" icon="plus">
    Click "New Space" on the dashboard. Name it (e.g., "Project Alpha Docs") and add your first table or page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Invite Collaborators" icon="mail">

</Steps>
